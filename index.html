About Me

I’m studying Sports Administration at Georgia State University, and a big part of why I’m passionate about it comes from being a D1 college athlete. Being in that environment every day has given me a firsthand look at everything that goes into sports beyond just playing, like leadership, organization, team culture, and what happens behind the scenes to keep everything running. It’s made me interested in the business and management side of athletics and how sports programs are built and supported. Since sports has always been such a huge part of my life, it just feels like the perfect field for me and something I can truly see myself building a future in.
